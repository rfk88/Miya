App Store Review — subscription & codes (paste into App Review Information / reply as needed)

1) In-App Purchase
- Subscription is implemented with StoreKit 2 only.
- Product ID: com.Miya_Health.monthly (auto-renewing subscription; free trial configured in App Store Connect).
- Paid Apps Agreement is accepted under the Account Holder; subscription group is attached to this app and the product is cleared for sale for testing/review.

2) “Join with code” vs subscription (Guideline 3.1.1)
- The “Join with code” / invite code on the sign-in screen is ONLY for joining an existing family account (invited members). It does not unlock or replace an App Store subscription.
- “Redeem offer code” on the subscription paywall opens Apple’s system offer code redemption UI (SwiftUI offerCodeRedemption). Codes are created in App Store Connect for this subscription; there is no custom server-side promo to unlock the subscription.

3) Family billing (if shown in-app)
- Any in-app “billing owner” / family billing prompts refer to internal family hub roles, not an alternative to the App Store subscription for digital content.

4) Testing
- Please test IAP with a Sandbox Apple ID. If the subscription sheet fails to load, use a network connection and retry; the paywall includes a “Try again” / “Retry” path when the App Store does not return the product.
- To test subscription offer codes: create sandbox offer codes in App Store Connect for com.Miya_Health.monthly, then on device (Sandbox account) use “Redeem offer code” on the paywall and enter a sandbox code.

Checklist for developers (App Store Connect — manual)
- Under the subscription product com.Miya_Health.monthly: add a Subscription Offer, then create offer codes (including sandbox codes for QA).
- Distribute test codes to QA; redeem from the paywall and confirm the paywall dismisses after StoreKit shows an active entitlement.
